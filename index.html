What is a Bufalien?


Bufalien Club is a collection of 10,000 randomly generated NFTs on the Polygon blockchain created by an NFT development group called The Middle Schoolers.

Our goal is to build a culture out of the intelligent gentle giant that Bufalien is. His image represents tranquility, being wise beyond his years, having unmatched strength, and habits that reflect profound generosity. These are the values that reflect on each Bufalien in the collection.

Not only do we want to spread these positive attribute to the new world of web 3.0, we want to make this kind of community available to anyone and everyone.

For the universally affordable price of 0.005 ETH, you'll obtain a unique Bufalien NFT that not only connects you with a growing community of Bufaliens, but also grants you exclusive access to IRL & Non-IRL events, NFT-specific merch, and news about our next big project: An NFT-inclusive Mars Colonization game featuring Bufalien.

Bufalien Roadmap
